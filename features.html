Why Choose Mindyourbusiness Villa for Your Stay?

At Mindyourbusiness Villa, we've thoughtfully curated our features to ensure you experience a comfortable, secure, and enjoyable stay in Abuja. We focus on providing the essentials you need with added benefits for a truly relaxing environment.

Comfortable Accommodation: Our well-appointed one and two-bedroom houses are designed for your relaxation.
Private Bathrooms: Each house features its own private bathroom with a refreshing shower.
In-Room Entertainment: Enjoy your favorite shows and movies on the television provided in each house.
Complimentary WiFi: Stay connected with our free WiFi available throughout the property.
Free Private Parking: Enjoy the convenience and security of our complimentary private parking.
Garden Views: Relax and enjoy the serene views from your house.
Optional Self-Catering: Select houses feature fully equipped kitchens for your convenience.
Recreational Facilities: Enjoy our football and basketball courts for some active fun.
Children's Play Park: We have a dedicated play area for our younger guests.
Cycling/Jogging Trail: Explore the surroundings and stay active on our dedicated trail.
Gated and Secure Estate: Your safety and peace of mind are our top priorities.
Private Entrance: Enjoy the privacy and convenience of your own entrance.
